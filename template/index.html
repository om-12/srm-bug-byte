Guidelines

  • You can donate directly to individual braveheart's account (upto max of ₹ 15 lakhs) or may donate to the Donate for Defenders corpus.
  • To ensure maximum coverage, a cap of ₹ 15 lakhs is envisaged per braveheart and the donor would be alerted if the amount exceeds ₹ 15 lakhs, so that they can choose to either decrease their contribution or divert part of the contribution to another bravehearts account, or to the Donate for Defenders corpus.
  • Donate for Defenders corpus would be managed by a commitee made up of eminent persons of repute and senior government officials, in equal number, who would decide to disburse the fund equitably to the bravehearts family on need basis.
